Uplifters browser plugin

Nice things happen too

Do you often feel down when you read news? Sometimes the world seems like it's going to hell 85% of the time... But there are so many people out there doing amazing things helping to solve many of our problems. How come we so rarely get to see these mentioned in news?

Uplifters (browser plugin) is here to take care of this and add some positive news into your daily digest.

You can install it for Brave, Chrome, Firefox and Edge browsers.

Enjoy!

Hi, my name is Marko. I'm a software developer and I love to experiment with different tools. Internet is getting difficult and I'm trying to improve small parts of it.

You can support my work on this on Patreon. (It takes several hours every week to look for good news and keep the code up-to date. And I don't even write any of the articles...)

Privacy policy of Uplifters can be found here.

In short, Uplifters don't use cookies but we do log
page visits, namely country, city and time.
